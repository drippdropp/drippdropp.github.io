Midpoint Formula

The mid-point formula is defined as,

(x1+x22,y1+y22)(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2})(2x1​+x2​​,2y1​+y2​​) 
function midpoint(u, v)
    [(u[k]+v[k])/2 for k in 1:2]
end

@show midpoint([1; 0], [0; 1])
> midpoint([1; 0], [0; 1]) = (0.5, 0.5)
