Multiples of 3 and 5 (PE01)

Multiples of 3 and 5, problem 01 on Project Euler, asks us to find the sum of all multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.

@time begin
    filter(k -> k % 3 == 0 || k % 5 == 0, 1:999) |> sum
end
